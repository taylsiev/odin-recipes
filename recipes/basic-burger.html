Basic Burger Recipe

Description:

This is a simple and delicious basic burger recipe that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheese
  • 4 leafs lettuce
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 4 slices onion

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Form the meat mixture into 4 patties.
  3. Grill or pan-fry the patties for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns if desired.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the bottom buns, adding lettuce, tomato, and onion slices.
